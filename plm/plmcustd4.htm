Doosan Heavy Industries & Construction Co., Ltd.
http://www.doosanheavy.com/

Since its establishment in 1962, Doosan Heavy Industries & Construction Co., Ltd. has been playing a major role in the development of Korea’s national economy by supplying industrial facilities to domestic and overseas markets.

Doosan’s field of business ranges from casting & forging to nuclear and hydro & thermal power plant construction, and the company is also deeply involved in the desalination plant, environmental and material handling sectors.

As the only Korean firm that specializes in power plants, Doosan is fully equipped with comprehensive production & supply systems, and manufactures from all base material to the finished product.Doosan has so far supplied a total of 160 power plant facilities with a combined force of 42,335MW, and is currently hard at work manufacturing the facility for 75power plant units (equivalent to 32,606MW).

In particular, Doosan is busy strengthening its international competitiveness in the field of power generation by developing made-in-Korea nuclear steam generators, reactor vessels and other nuclear power related systems. Its recent achievements include the Taean Thermal Plant and other 500MW Korean standard thermal power plants.

Furthermore, Doosan has advanced into the international nuclear power market by securing projects for Qinshan of China and sequoyah, U.S. ? thus launching a new era of nuclear power facilities export for Korea.
